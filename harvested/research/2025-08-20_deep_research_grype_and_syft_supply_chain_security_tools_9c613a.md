## Open Source tools for container security.
![Logo: Syft by Anchore](https://cdn-dllid.nitrocdn.com/lfaFdmeTaONuMeYAvisIiEiZRrNJpVpd/assets/images/optimized/rev-67c0a14/anchore.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/12/Frame-1544.png)
Generate a comprehensive Software Bill of Materials (SBOM) with our CLI tool, Syft.
![](https://cdn-dllid.nitrocdn.com/lfaFdmeTaONuMeYAvisIiEiZRrNJpVpd/assets/images/optimized/rev-67c0a14/anchore.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/folder-details-1.png)
Gain visibility down to the file level.
![](https://cdn-dllid.nitrocdn.com/lfaFdmeTaONuMeYAvisIiEiZRrNJpVpd/assets/images/optimized/rev-67c0a14/anchore.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/autoscaling-1.png)
Automatically generate SBOMs in your CI/CD pipeline.
![](https://cdn-dllid.nitrocdn.com/lfaFdmeTaONuMeYAvisIiEiZRrNJpVpd/assets/images/optimized/rev-67c0a14/anchore.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/column-dependency-1-1.png)
Uncover direct and transitive dependencies.
![](https://cdn-dllid.nitrocdn.com/lfaFdmeTaONuMeYAvisIiEiZRrNJpVpd/assets/images/optimized/rev-67c0a14/anchore.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/document-1.png)
Output SBOMs in JSON, SPDX, and CycloneDX formats.
[Download on GitHub](https://github.com/anchore/syft/)
[Learn More](https://anchore.com/sbom/how-to-generate-an-sbom-with-free-open-source-tools/)
![Logo: Grype by Anchore](https://cdn-dllid.nitrocdn.com/lfaFdmeTaONuMeYAvisIiEiZRrNJpVpd/assets/images/optimized/rev-67c0a14/anchore.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/12/Frame-1544-1.png)
Generate a list of known vulnerabilities from an SBOM, container image, or project directory with our CLI tool, Grype.
![](https://cdn-dllid.nitrocdn.com/lfaFdmeTaONuMeYAvisIiEiZRrNJpVpd/assets/images/optimized/rev-67c0a14/anchore.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/zoom-fit-2-1.png)
Scan OS and language-specific packages.
![](https://cdn-dllid.nitrocdn.com/lfaFdmeTaONuMeYAvisIiEiZRrNJpVpd/assets/images/optimized/rev-67c0a14/anchore.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/flow-logs-vpc-1-1.png)
View optimized results across vulnerability sources.
![](https://cdn-dllid.nitrocdn.com/lfaFdmeTaONuMeYAvisIiEiZRrNJpVpd/assets/images/optimized/rev-67c0a14/anchore.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/autoscaling-1-1.png)
Automate scans in your CI/CD pipeline.
![](https://cdn-dllid.nitrocdn.com/lfaFdmeTaONuMeYAvisIiEiZRrNJpVpd/assets/images/optimized/rev-67c0a14/anchore.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/point-of-presence-1.png)
Combine with Syft for faster scans.
