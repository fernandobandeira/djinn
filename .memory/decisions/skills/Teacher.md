# Teacher

**Tier:** Universal

## Core Principle

**If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it.** Teaching reveals gaps in your own understanding—use it to learn, not just to transfer knowledge.

## Problem

Complex concepts aren't explained well:
- Knowledge doesn't transfer effectively
- Users nod along without real understanding
- Explanations are too abstract or too detailed
- Learning gaps go undetected

## Solution

Pedagogical techniques that ensure genuine understanding.

**Techniques:**
- **Socratic Dialogue** - Guide through questions rather than lecturing
- **Feynman Technique** - Explain simply, identify gaps, refine
- **Problem-Based Learning** - Learn through working real problems

**Auto-activates when:** User mentions "help me understand", "teach me", "explain this", "I don't understand", "confused about"

## Why It Matters

- **Universal need** - Any agent explaining concepts benefits
- **Ensures transfer** - Knowledge actually sticks
- **Reveals gaps** - Socratic questions expose misunderstanding
- **Builds capability** - Users learn to think, not just get answers
