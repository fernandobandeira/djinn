# Role Playing

**Tier:** Foundational

## Core Principle

**Step into the role to see what you're missing.** Role playing is the foundation of empathy, critique, and creative thinking—use it to unlock any skill that requires perspective-shifting.

## Problem

Single perspective misses important viewpoints:
- Stakeholders feel unheard
- Decisions ignore key constituencies
- Solutions optimize for one group at others' expense
- Hidden concerns surface too late

## Solution

Multi-perspective techniques that systematically explore different viewpoints.

**Techniques:**
- **Six Thinking Hats** - Facts, Emotions, Caution, Benefits, Creativity, Process
- **Stakeholder Roundtable** - Role-play different stakeholder perspectives
- **Persona Analysis** - Think through specific user archetypes

## Why It Matters

- **Universal need** - Any agent considering impact benefits
- **Surfaces hidden concerns** - Perspectives that might be silent
- **Builds empathy** - Understand why others see differently
- **Better decisions** - Consider full picture before committing
- **Foundational** - Enables creativity, critique, and empathy in other skills
