# Product Management Elicitation Methods

## Core Techniques
1. User Interviews
2. Surveys
3. Contextual Inquiry
4. Focus Groups
5. Market Research

## Advanced Elicitation Strategies
- Persona Development

## Success Criteria for Elicitation
- Clarity of User Needs
- Actionable Insights
- Comprehensive Coverage
- Minimal Bias
- Measurable Outcomes
