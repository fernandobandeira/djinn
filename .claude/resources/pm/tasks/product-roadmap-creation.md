# Product Roadmap Creation Task

## Objective
Develop a comprehensive, strategic product roadmap that aligns business goals with user needs.

## Key Steps
1. Review existing strategic objectives
2. Analyze market research
3. Gather stakeholder input
4. Prioritize features and initiatives
5. Create timeline with milestones
6. Define OKRs for each phase

## Success Criteria
- Alignment with business strategy
- Clear, measurable milestones
- Stakeholder buy-in
- Flexible and adaptable plan
- Quantifiable success metrics

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid
- Over-committing resources
- Ignoring market feedback
- Lack of flexibility
- Missing clear success metrics
