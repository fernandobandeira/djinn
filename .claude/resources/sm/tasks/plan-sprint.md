# Plan Sprint Task

## Prerequisites
- Groomed backlog
- Team capacity known
- Previous sprint retrospective reviewed

## Workflow
1. Load Backlog
   - Source: /docs/backlog/backlog.md
   - Filter by priority and readiness

2. Capacity Calculation
   - Use: cognitive-tools/sprint-velocity-calculator.md
   - Assess team availability
   - Determine sprint capacity

3. Story Selection
   - Apply story-estimation-framework.md
   - Match stories to team capacity
   - Ensure balanced workload

4. Sprint Plan Generation
   - Output: /docs/sprints/{year}.{month}.{sprint}.md
   - Include:
     * Selected stories
     * Story points
     * Team assignments
     * Sprint goal

5. Post-Planning
   - Notify team
   - Update story statuses
   - Prepare daily standup schedule

## Validation Checklist
- [ ] Backlog loaded
- [ ] Team capacity calculated
- [ ] Stories selected
- [ ] Sprint plan generated
- [ ] Team notified
- [ ] Standup prepared
