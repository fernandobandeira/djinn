## Risk Mitigation

### Critical Risks & Responses
1. **Low adoption (<5% scanners)**
   - Pivot to email-first strategy
   - Increase rewards/gamification
   - Partner with existing app

2. **Fast competitor copying**
   - Accelerate unique features
   - Focus on brand/community
   - Consider acquisition talks

3. **Technical issues (OCR accuracy)**
   - Multiple OCR provider backup
   - Manual correction options
   - Crowdsourced improvements

4. **Privacy breach**
   - Insurance from Day 1
   - Regular security audits
   - Incident response plan

5. **CAC exceeds LTV**
   - Optimize conversion funnel
   - Increase retention focus
   - Add revenue streams
