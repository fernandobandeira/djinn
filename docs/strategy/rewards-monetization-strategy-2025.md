## Executive Summary

This document outlines the complete rewards strategy for Djinn's **mandatory trial-to-premium model**, designed to achieve 48.8% trial conversion and 20-36% weekly active scanning rate by delivering $5-10 monthly value through the "Lamp Points" system. Users must sign up for a 7-day trial with payment information to access the app.

## Business Model: Mandatory Trial-to-Premium

### No Free Tier Access
- **Entry Requirement**: 7-day trial signup with payment method
- **Trial Benefits**: 500 starter points + full premium features
- **Conversion Target**: 48.8% trial-to-paid
- **Premium Price**: $7.99/month or $59/year

## Core Principle: Points Drive Engagement & Conversion
**Exchange Rate**: 1,000 points = $1.00 perceived value
**Purpose**: Create habit loops during trial, drive premium conversion

# Psychological Design Elements

## Variable Ratio Rewards (Gambling Psychology)
- Random "Golden Receipts" worth 10x points
- Surprise bonuses for scanning certain merchants
- Mystery boxes at point thresholds
- Scratch cards for bonus points

## Loss Aversion (Behavioral Economics)
- Points expire after 6 months (drives redemption)
- "Streak at risk" notifications
- Limited-time 2x redemption events
- "Use it or lose it" monthly bonuses

## Social Proof (FOMO)
- "2,847 users redeemed this reward today"
- "Only 100 left this month"
- Lamp Circle group rewards
- Leaderboards with anonymized rankings

## Progress Mechanics (Gaming Psychology)
- Visual progress bars to next reward
- "Only 243 points to your next wish!"
- Tier status progression:
  - Lamp Holder (0-10K lifetime points)
  - Wish Maker (10K-50K)
  - Genie Commander (50K-200K)
  - Genie Master (200K+)

# Economic Model

## Cost Structure
| Redemption Type | Cost per 1000 Points | Margin |
|-----------------|---------------------|---------|
| AI Wishes | $0.10 (API costs) | 90% |
| OCR Power-Ups | $0.02 (processing) | 98% |
| Premium Trials | $0.00 (opportunity cost) | 100% |
| Gift Cards | $1.10 (includes fees) | -10% |
| Cash Back | $1.05 (transfer fees) | -5% |
| Merchant Discounts | $0.80 (negotiated) | 20% |

## Sustainability Requirements
- Average redemption cost: <$0.60 per 1000 points
- Premium conversion from trials: >5%
- User lifetime value: >$50
- Breakeven: Month 8-10

## Funding Requirements
- Initial rewards pool: $50K
- Monthly rewards budget: $10-20K
- Growing to: $100K/month at 100K users

- Redemption caps
- Reserved reward pools

- Simple redemption process
- Instant gratification options
