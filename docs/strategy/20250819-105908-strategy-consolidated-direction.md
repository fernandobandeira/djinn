## Engagement System

### Trial-to-Premium Model
**No Free Tier**: 7-day mandatory trial with payment info required
- **Trial Points**: 500 starter points to experience AI wishes
- **Trial Features**: Full premium access for 7 days
- **Conversion Target**: 48.8% trial-to-paid at $7.99/month

### Points-Driven Wish Loop
**Core Mechanic**: Earn Points ‚Üí Make AI Wishes ‚Üí Get Value ‚Üí Convert to Premium

**AI Wish Types & Costs**:
1. **Quick Analysis** (200 pts): "What's my biggest expense?"
2. **Savings Finder** (300 pts): "Find subscriptions to cancel"
3. **Comparison** (400 pts): "Am I overpaying for groceries?"
4. **Tax Helper** (2,000 pts): "Find all tax deductions"
5. **Investment Advisor** (3,000 pts): "Analyze for investment potential"

**Points System**:
- **Trial Users**: 100 points per receipt scan, 500 starter bonus
- **Premium Users**: 200 points per scan, 1,000 monthly allowance
- No pay-to-win - points earned through engagement only

### Points Shop & Rewards

**Points Economy**:
- Earned through receipt scanning and engagement
- Monthly allowance for premium members (1,000 pts)
- Non-monetary rewards during MVP phase

**Primary Redemptions (Trial & Premium)**:
- 200-500 pts: Basic AI wishes during trial
- 500 pts: Extra AI wish token
- 1,000 pts: Bulk OCR processing (50 receipts)
- 1,500 pts: Tax-ready export reports
- 2,000 pts: Premium AI wishes
- 2,000 pts: Priority support (1 month)
- 3,000 pts: Advanced financial analysis

**Status Levels**:
1. Apprentice (0-1K points lifetime)
2. Saver (1K-5K points)
3. Master (5K-15K points)
4. Legend (15K+ points)

## Launch Strategy

### Messaging
**Hero Message**: "Your money wishes, granted."
**Supporting**: "The only finance app that actually shows what's in your receipts"

### Acquisition Channels
1. **Displaced Mint Users**: Direct migration tools and comparison content
2. **Gen Z Social**: TikTok/Instagram finance creators showing receipt reveals
3. **Reddit**: r/personalfinance, r/budget, college subreddits
4. **Referral Program**: "Grant a wish" - both users get points

### Content Strategy
- Receipt reveal videos ("What's really in my Target haul")
- Djinn Wrapped shares driving FOMO
- "Djinn vs Others" comparison content
- Success stories without revealing amounts

## Key Success Factors

### Must Nail
1. **OCR Accuracy**: 90%+ accuracy on receipt scanning
2. **Djinn Personality**: Consistent, funny, never annoying
3. **Privacy Protection**: Zero data leaks, clear controls
4. **Wish Algorithm**: Personalized, achievable, engaging
5. **Onboarding**: First wish completed within 5 minutes

### Differentiation Moats
1. **Receipt Intelligence Database**: Proprietary product/price data
2. **Djinn Brand Identity**: Unique personality in sterile fintech space
3. **Community Points System**: Engagement without financial exposure
4. **Wish Personalization**: AI that truly knows user's patterns

### Risk Mitigation
- **High Churn**: Continuous wish loop maintains engagement
- **OCR Adoption**: Gamification makes scanning fun, not work
- **Privacy Concerns**: Transparent data use, user controls
- **Competition**: First-mover on receipts + strong brand identity
