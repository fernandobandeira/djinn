## Engagement System

### On-Demand Wish Loop
**Core Mechanic**: Complete Wish ‚Üí Earn Points ‚Üí AI generates new personalized wish ‚Üí Repeat

**Wish Types**:
1. **Quick Wins** (5-10 min): "Scan 3 receipts" = 50 points
2. **Discovery** (instant): "Find most expensive subscription" = 100 points
3. **Challenges** (1-3 days): "Reduce delivery spending 20%" = 300 points
4. **Learning** (2 min): "Categorize 10 transactions" = 75 points
5. **Optimization** (varies): "Cancel 1 unused subscription" = 200 points

**Cooldown System**:
- **Free Users**: Big wishes every 72 hours, unlimited small wishes
- **Premium Users**: Big wishes every 24 hours, unlimited small wishes
- No streak pressure - engagement at user's own pace

### Points & Rewards

**Points Economy**:
- Points earned through wishes only (no pay-to-win)
- Non-monetary rewards maintain trust

**Redemption Options**:
- 300 pts: Djinn costume changes
- 500 pts: 10 extra OCR scans
- 600 pts: Animated reactions
- 1000 pts: Early feature access
- 1500 pts: Priority support (1 month)
- 2000 pts: Unlimited custom categories

**Status Levels**:
1. Apprentice (0-1K points)
2. Saver (1K-5K points)
3. Master (5K-15K points)
4. Legend (15K+ points)

### Messaging

### Acquisition Channels
1. **Displaced Mint Users**: Direct migration tools and comparison content
2. **Gen Z Social**: TikTok/Instagram finance creators showing receipt reveals
3. **Reddit**: r/personalfinance, r/budget, college subreddits
4. **Referral Program**: "Grant a wish" - both users get points

### Content Strategy
- Receipt reveal videos ("What's really in my Target haul")
- Djinn Wrapped shares driving FOMO
- "Djinn vs Others" comparison content
- Success stories without revealing amounts

## Key Success Factors

### Must Nail
1. **OCR Accuracy**: 90%+ accuracy on receipt scanning
2. **Djinn Personality**: Consistent, funny, never annoying
3. **Privacy Protection**: Zero data leaks, clear controls
4. **Wish Algorithm**: Personalized, achievable, engaging
5. **Onboarding**: First wish completed within 5 minutes

### Differentiation Moats
1. **Receipt Intelligence Database**: Proprietary product/price data
2. **Djinn Brand Identity**: Unique personality in sterile fintech space
3. **Community Points System**: Engagement without financial exposure
4. **Wish Personalization**: AI that truly knows user's patterns

### Risk Mitigation
- **High Churn**: Continuous wish loop maintains engagement
- **OCR Adoption**: Gamification makes scanning fun, not work
- **Privacy Concerns**: Transparent data use, user controls
- **Competition**: First-mover on receipts + strong brand identity
