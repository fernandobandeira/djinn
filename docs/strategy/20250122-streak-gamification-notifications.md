## Implementation Guidelines

### Notification Timing
- **Streak Reminders:** 8-9 PM local time
- **Achievement Unlocks:** Immediate
- **Challenge Updates:** Optimal engagement windows
- **Point Earnings:** Batched if multiple within 30 min

### Frequency Caps
- Max 1 streak reminder per day
- Max 2 achievement notifications per day
- Challenge updates: 2 per week max
- Point notifications: Batch after 3+ actions

### A/B Testing Variables
- Streak reminder timing (7 PM vs 8 PM vs 9 PM)
- Message framing (loss vs gain)
- Emoji usage in achievements
- Time Traveler personality level

## Success Metrics

### Target Outcomes
- **7-day streak:** 50% of users
- **30-day streak:** 20% of users
- **66-day streak:** 10% of users
- **Streak recovery rate:** 30% after break
- **Achievement share rate:** 15% of major milestones
- **Challenge participation:** 25% of active users

### Engagement Indicators
- Notification CTR: >45% for streaks
- Achievement view rate: >60%
- Points redemption: 40% monthly
- Freeze usage: <5% of streak days

## Risk Mitigation

### Avoiding Burnout
- Vacation mode for planned breaks
- Streak freeze for emergencies
- Reduced pressure after 66 days
- No shame in streak breaks

### Preventing Anxiety
- Positive framing of breaks
- Multiple recovery options
- Focus on progress not perfection
- Celebrate comebacks

### Managing Competition
- Optional social features
- Private achievement options
- No public failure broadcasting
- Cooperative challenges available
